September 2025 Visit — Innovation Research Readout
✓ Delivered
During Vation's September 2025 onsite visit to Christensen Farms, two research deliverables were presented to the Innovation Team: a comprehensive Oracle OCI technology landscape and roadmap, and a set of six 2026 Innovation Themes to guide the committee's annual initiative planning. These documents established the strategic foundation that all subsequent workstreams have been built against.

Oracle OCI Phases
Deliverable 1 — Oracle OCI & Technology Landscape
  • Observability & Decisioning: Immediate opportunity for data-driven benchmarking, process mapping, and cost-benefit impact modeling across all operations
  • Sensors & Animal Wellbeing: Thermal cameras, CV algorithms, and edge-deployed sensors for feed, water, manure, and movement monitoring — MAXIMUS/AP infrastructure immediately actionable
  • Acoustic Monitoring: Pig vocalizations as health indicators — respiratory and digestive disease detection via microphone arrays above pens and feeding areas
  • Robotic Lawnmowing: $20k estimated annual manual lawnmowing cost (5-acre facility, 39 visits/yr at $50/worker/acre) vs. $5k–$15k projected savings from autonomous robotic systems
  • Air Filtration & Purification: Electrostatic and catalytic solutions evaluated for pathogen reduction across transport and fixed barn environments
  • Frontline Enablement: Remote risk nudges, task orchestration, and automated transcription identified as high-priority operational improvements
Deliverable 2 — 2026 Innovation Themes
  • Consistency: Deliver predictable pig flow with stable live births, weights, and outputs across all production sites
  • Observability: Gain real-time visibility into health, safety, and operations through data and technology investments
  • Connectivity: Seamlessly link people, systems, and processes for faster, smarter operational decisions
  • Throughput & Efficiency: Increase production output while lowering cost and labor without sacrificing quality
  • Safety & Well-Being: Eliminate safety incidents and enhance animal care standards across all operations
  • Sustainability & Traceability: Reduce resource use and strengthen animal and food transparency for compliance and consumer trust
Oracle OCI Roadmap — Three-Phase Architecture
  • Phase 1 — Data Platform & Observability: Unify internal and external data management; leverage OCI Data Platform (lakehouse, RAG, vector search) and pre-trained CV models for movement/detection; Fusion-integrated OCR for document processing
  • Phase 2 — IoT & Sensor Integration: Centralize system observability; connect IoT apps to real-time telemetry from sensors; link Fusion ERP data to asset monitoring, diagnostics, and triggered maintenance workflows
  • Phase 3 — AI Orchestration & Digital Twins: Real-time multi-modal CV intelligence across behavior and acoustics; agentic AI automation of procurement, inventory, and logistics via Fusion SCM; digital twinning of facilities and pens to simulate and optimize production scenarios
  • Animal Welfare Score Index: Combining CV, environmental, sensor, and asset data into a composite index supporting multi-modal AI, threshold alerting, and predictive intelligence — natural language querying enabled ("Show barns with rising CO₂, low feed intake, and declining weight")
